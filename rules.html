The game consists of two levels:


1- The character you are going to check will be Superman and you will have to recover
all his 5 "S" to reach the second level.

2- The last level is the most difficult one, because you will have a more limited control of Iron Man. To finish the game you will have to recover all 5 gems of infinity.
